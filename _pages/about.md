I am an Research Assistant Professor at the [Shenyang Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://sia.cas.cn/). His research focuses on robot vision and industrial artificial intelligence. His work has been published in top-tier journals and conferences, including CVPR, AAAI, TIP, TNNLS, TMM, TCSVT, and TIV. He has led several projects, including the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation, the Shandong Laboratory of Aluminum Advanced Manufacturing in Binzhou Open Project, the Fundamental Research Project of SIA, and the open-source project [**MulimgViewer**](https://github.com/nachifur/MulimgViewer) (a multi-image viewer with 1.4k stars on GitHub and over 12,000 downloads).

He serves as a reviewer for top-tier international journals and conferences such as IEEE TPAMI, TIP, TNNLS, TMM, TCSVT, TII, Information Fusion, Neurocomputing, SPL, CVPR 2026, ICML 2026, ICLR 2026, AAAI 2026, ECCV 2026, NeurIPS 2025, ICCV 2025, WACV 2026, ECCV 2022, ICIRA 2022, and Image and Vision Computing. He is also a member of the Visualization and Cognitive Computing Committee of the China Graphics Society, a committee member of CCF YOCSEF Shenyang, and a Young Editorial Board Member of Intelligence & Robotics.

He has received several awards, including the First Prize in the 2nd Liaoning Provincial Postdoctoral Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, the Best Poster Award at IEEE-CYBER 2023, the Innovation Award in the Liaoning Youth AI Innovation Competition, and the Third Prize in the 4th China Open Source Innovation Competition.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. [Yandong Tang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CaglmKAAAAAJ&hl=en).
